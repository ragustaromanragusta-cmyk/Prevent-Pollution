10 Practical Ways to Prevent Pollution

♻️

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Buy less, choose reusable items, and recycle properly. Sort your waste and use recycling bins correctly.

🚫

Say No to Single-Use Plastics

Carry reusable bags, water bottles, coffee cups, and straws. Refuse plastic bags at stores.

🚗

Choose Green Transportation

Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transit. Consider electric vehicles to cut emissions.

💡

Save Energy at Home

Use LED bulbs, unplug devices, wash with cold water, and set thermostat to 68°F (20°C).

🌱

Eat More Plants

Reduce meat consumption. Choose local, seasonal produce to lower transportation emissions.

💧

Conserve Water

Fix leaks, take shorter showers, install low-flow fixtures, and only run full loads.
